Introducing Gabby Santas

Hi, my name is Gabby! I grew up in the Bay Area, California, and I love line dancing, reading contemporary fiction, and doing the crossword puzzle. I'm interested in narrative journalism, specifically documentary film and audio. I'm excited to report on a wide and random range of topics from health care, migration/diaspora, drug policy, LGBTQ culture, and more...
